Organization

The group has over 135 members loosely organized across our social networks. The five current organizers are:

  • Jeremy Shaw
  • Luke Hoersten
  • David Johnson
  • Matthew Wraith
  • Reid Draper

History

Chicago Haskell was started in 2009 by Jeremy Shaw. Other members like Luke Hoersten joined later that year and the group grew from there. The group existed primarily on the mailing list and IRC to begin with and moved into Google+ and Meetup as the platforms gained popularity.
